Around the Web

Japan Plank

The Japan Plank project was meant as a gift for a newly started branch of Eon Reality, in Japan. It mimiced the popular VR plank experience, with a couple of minigames for flavor.

Quest Phobias

Quest Phobias was a project that aimed to treat different kinds of phobias through exposure therapy. Initially, it aimed to treat acrophobia and claustrophobia.
A cool feature of the project was that we got an Oculus Quest to communicate with an Arduino and a fitnes tracker over Bluetooth, so that we could react to a user's actual heartrate.
It was meant to be part of Odense Techblast 2020, which was cancelled due to Covid-19.

Lego World VR

My bachelors project was an attempt to bring the LEGO brand into VR. We collaborated with LEGO, where they provided models and sparring on occasion. We created a proof of concept for a networked open-world system, that included specially designed locomotion systems.
